What are the advantages of AWS?
With AWS, you receive a virtual environment that lets you load the software and services your application requires. This eases the migration process for existing applications while preserving options for building new solutions.


AWS Benefits and Advantages. User-friendly. Flexible. Secure. Cost-effective. Reliable. Scalable and Elastic. Highly Performant.
Disadvantages of AWS. Limitations. Lack of Experts. Price Variations. General Issues.
